About Puerto Rico's Hurricane Georges

Before Hurricane Georges, the last hurricane to hit Puerto Rico was in 1932. On September 15, 2017 this devastating hurricane formed off the African coast and became a category 4 hurricane when it hit the island. This devastating storm took eight lives and cost Puerto Rico $2 million in damages. Three different tornados were reported and flooding caused countless rivers to overflow. The county is estimated to have lost 65% of its poultry and more than 50% of its crops. While 72,000 houses were partially destroyed, 28,005 houses were utterly destroyed by this hurricane.Here, at Give and Get, you can help by volunteering to help the victims of Hurricane Georges. No matter how small, your donation will help those in need.
